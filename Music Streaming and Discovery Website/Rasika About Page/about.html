Welcome to Rasika: Where Royaly Meets Music
In the vibrant world of digital music, Rasika stands as a beacon of innovation and passion.
This music streaming platform, co-founded by three friends – Sumit, Sushaant, and Partha –
embarked on its journey in 2015 with a singular vision: to celebrate the rich tapestry of Indian classical music and dance.
