Biography

Jubin Nautiyal, born on June 14, 1989, is an Indian playback singer and performer. He is known for his soul-stirring voice and has lent his vocals to numerous hit songs in Bollywood. Jubin's versatility and emotive singing style have garnered him widespread acclaim.
