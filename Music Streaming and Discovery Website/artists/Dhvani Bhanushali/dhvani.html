Biography

Dhvani Bhanushali, born on March 2, 1988, is an Indian singer and songwriter. She rose to fame with her debut single 'Vaaste,' which became one of the most-viewed Indian songs on YouTube. Dhvani is known for her soulful voice and versatile singing style.
