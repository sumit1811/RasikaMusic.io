Arijit Singh

The Soulful Voice of Bollywood

Biography

Arijit Singh, born on April 25, 1987, is an Indian playback singer. He is one of the most versatile and soulful voices in the Bollywood music industry. Singh gained fame with his debut song 'Phir Mohabbat' and has since delivered numerous chart-topping hits.
