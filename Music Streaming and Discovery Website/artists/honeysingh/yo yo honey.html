Biography

Yo Yo Honey Singh, born on March 15, 1983, is an Indian music producer, pop singer, and film actor. He is one of the highest-paid music producers in Bollywood. Singh started as a session and recording artist and became a Bhangra music producer. He is known for his energetic and peppy music, blending Punjabi and Western sounds.
